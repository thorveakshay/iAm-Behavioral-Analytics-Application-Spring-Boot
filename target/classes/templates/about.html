About Project



It is hard to think of a domain that is going to change the world as much as Software and Artificial Intelligence (AI) will, in the decades ahead. Also, according to Moore’s law “Overall processing power for computers will double every two years.” We already achieved goal of Web 1.0 i.e. connect the all population together. We did that using Internet, social media, web pages, forums etc. Also, we are working on Web 2.0 to achieve its goal of connecting all devices together and we are doing this using The Internet of things (IoT), Artificial Intelligence (AI), machine learning etc. Everything is good, and we will see much more progress in all these things in coming decades.

I am always with evolving technology, but I think we are missing most Important thing in our life and that is nothing but ourselves. We are more interested in other people’s lives. We use Facebook, Instagram, Snapchat and many more to mention to look in to other people’s life. Mostly we do it passively, just check their profile, time line or news feeds. We want to know where they are going, what they are doing, what they are interested in what they are eating and all such things. I know that’s important in some cases, but we got one life its always beneficial to look in our life, analyze it, improve it, find ways which suites our personality.

That’s the reason I want to build something which will help people analyze, review themselves. Find out their personality type using some unique questions and possibly algorithms. Suggest connecting similar minded persons. Also generate some personalized tips to improve, refer some commercial books/products to them. Also, try to show some relevant news and articles to them using google feeds etc. Basically, make them love and focus on themselves.
